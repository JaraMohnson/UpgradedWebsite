Welcome to My Website :)

I am thrilled to be starting fresh with new goals and a new career path. I've spent many years bartending, and had a great ride at Rocket Mortgage from 2019-2022. I'm here to break into tech to utilize my creativity and have endless growth potential and paths to take.

Why Am I Here?

My current developer goals are to finish my current C#/.NET bootcamp successfully, and then pursue further skillsets/languages, whether that be with a future employer or self-studies. I chose C# for its powerful capabilities as well as its versatility. I have family in the industry who work in client-facing, project management roles, and back-end developement. They were a huge part of the push to get me to pull the trigger and start on my own path. My uncle told me about Python about 5 years ago and said "if you ever want to talk to me about this, I am all ears." After that, it had been a lingering thought in my mind, but self-doubt crept on me for so long that I just hadn't STARTED. Once I started, when the science field called me back where I belong after 3 years in finance, I went all in.

Where Do I Live?

I grew up in Michigan, born and raised. There isn't much exciting to say about my actual hometown in Mid-Michigan, but I currently live in Metro Detroit with other small roots in St. Helen, MI and Sarasota, FL. My goal is to be able to work remotely, and live in a few cities before I find my forever home. The ocean is what calls me the most, so I'm positive I'll end up near the water somewhere in the US.
